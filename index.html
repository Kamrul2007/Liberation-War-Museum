Eternal Flame of Freedom

Preserving the history and honoring the heroes of 1971. A sanctuary for
memories that shaped the birth of Bangladesh.

Our Sacred Duty

Founded in 1996, the Bangladesh Liberation War Museum stands as a
testament to the resilience of the human spirit. Our mission is to ocument
the 1971 Genocide and the heroic struggle for independence, ensuring that
the sacrifices of 3 million martyrs and the dignity of 200,000 women are
never forgotten.

Through more than 21,000 artifacts, personal archives, and survivors'
accounts, we provide a platform for historical truth. We serve not just as a
repository of the past, but as a beacon for democratic values, secularism,
and universal human rights for future generations.
